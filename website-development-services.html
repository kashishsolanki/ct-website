Our Approach

1. Requirement Understanding

The very first and most important stage is where our experts stay in constant communication with all the stakeholders/client for making sure that our identification of requirements matches with what you want

2. Feasibility Checking

After requirement gathering, our talented engineers try to find and get alternative solutions to all the holdbacks for smoothing the website development process

3. Design Prototyping & Wireframing

Once we are over with the understanding of the whole idea, our creative designers start making the flow and wireframes of the website, where we keep our clients in the loop to get the best designs that are tailor-made for them

4. Content Gathering and Development

We start with the visuals and content generation after approval of wireframes and custom-made designs. After that, our tech team uses the latest tools and plugins for making the stable and enriched with the latest feature.

5. Quality Assurance

We run multiple test checks on the developed website in the beta stage to make the bug-free site. In addition, our responsive website development process includes test cases for looking after how the website will look on different devices.

6. Launch and Maintenance

When the website is completely ready and gets the green flag from QA, we set the strategy for the website launch. We take care of the hosting, domain set up, and security of the website. Our support team stays in touch with the client 24*7 to avoid any uncertain events.
