Tachome.

Taking the smart-home concept to the next level, the Tachome mobile application features a very simple UX design that is intuitive for any member of the family to use.

02

Clan Only.

For rapid login and security, we've included a Face ID feature in the app. The Tachome app will be accessible only to those who have been granted access.

03

One for All.

The application allows users to control every device and gadget in their smart home, as well as add devices to each room of the house.

04

Weather Check.

We've integrated temperature and weather updates in the app so that you may modify all of your home gadgets suitably.

05

Set it Up.

The application was incorporated with the feature to allow you to easily schedule the on/off time of the devices and also control the power of the lights.
