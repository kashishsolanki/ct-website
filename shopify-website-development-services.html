What is Shopify Development?

Shopify is a popular eCommerce platform to set up an online store for your business. Shopify sells retail point-of-sale (POS) software for brick & mortar models. Achieve splendid global expansion for your business with a custom theme Shopify web design.

The endless suite of features with Shopify development services will aid in managing various business activities like product sourcing, shipping, sales & inventory tracking, customer accounts, marketing & reporting.

Why should you invest in Shopify development?

Secure & Reliable

As an eCommerce platform, you have plenty of customer information, more importantly, the payment details stored are critical. By collaborating with a Shopify development company, you can ensure that your site stays secure and online 24/7.

Mobile ready

All the themes with Shopify web development are responsive and include a free built-in mobile commerce shopping cart establishing a unified shopping experience.

Customizable

With plenty of mobile-responsive Shopify themes available and the freedom to embed custom codes with each theme is all you need to build a fully-customized website reflecting your brand.

Shopify App store

Shopify has its own app that allows you to manage your store on the go from anywhere. Plus, you get access to plenty of apps in the store for improving the features of your Shopify website.

SEO & marketing tools

Optimize your Shopify website with a wide range of marketing tools on the app store like social media integration, email marketing & more to reach the target customers. Shopify also avails of advanced analytics tools to see where your customers are coming from.

Abandoned cart recovery

Shopify has a splendid solution for abandoned carts, the abandoned cart recovery tool sends emails to users as a reminder of their incomplete purchases to increase conversion rates.

Own payment gateway

To avail payment flexibility there are dozens of payment solutions available to choose from for fast, secure, and smooth transactions for customers.
